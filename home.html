I specialize in making windows and doors to match the existing in old buildings. All work is custom designed, from the sizes of the elements to the muntin profiles. Typical jobs require matching new window sash to old jambs, or new doors to old entrances.

For new construction, I make traditional windows and doors that work with period designs.
