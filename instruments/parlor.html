Parlor

This guitar was designed with comfort, playability and portability as top priorities. With 20 frets and a full length scale, the sound-hole sits centered just below the waist with the bridge resting towards the bottom of the lower bout. Due to it's light bracing and active back, the sound tends to be ambient and full with excellent high end clarity and low end response.

Specifications
  • Depth at heel: 3 5/32"
  • Depth at end block: 3 1/4"
  • upper bout: 9 1/2"
  • Waist : 7 13/16"
  • lower bout: 13 3/4"
  • Sound hole: 3 7/8"
  • Body Length: 17 5/8"
